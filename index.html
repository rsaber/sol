Why the calendar is broken

What does Sol do differently?

Given approx. 365 Earth rotations in a full orbit around the Sun, the Sol calendar creates 13 months of 28 days, and 1 day-less, date-less and month-less day (an extra on leap years).

Why is it better?

The Sol calendar ensures that each month is exactly 4 weeks long, and the nth of any month will always land on the same day, regardless of the month or year.
